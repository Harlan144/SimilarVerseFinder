About Similar Verse Finder

This website was created by the Piccolo Lab.

Our goal is to facilitate dialogue between religions and to help members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints and other religions to compare scriptures and find novel similarities for personal religious study. To use this tool, users may select a verse from the Standard Works (Old Testament, New Testament, Book of Mormon, Doctrine and Covenants, or Pearl of Great Price) or input a prompt/sentence of their choice. The five most semantically similar verses (as ranked by our machine-learning model) will be returned.

Our approach relies on language models to find semantic similarities between texts. More specifically, it uses transformer models and compares verses using the Cosine similarity metric. We rely on the Hugging Face and Sentence Transformers tools to perform these tasks. More information about the development of this project can be found at our GitHub site.
